Dear Parents,

My name is Cara, and I have been a speech-language pathologist for the past twenty-five years. The idea of Peanut Butter Panda has evolved from my work with children of all ages. It is my dream for your children to fall in love with Peanut Butter Panda and his friends. I hope these characters will be able to help teach your children the value of family and how to be a good friend.
